# Product Sense

## Mission

**Help & Grow** is the **AI Native Expert Network** for people building in **Singapore and Southeast Asia** (and beyond): find the right expertise, book real sessions, and earn recognition and tokens when you help others.

## Vision: service as agent

We are building toward **service as agent**: each expert can have a **digital counterpart** that **continuously learns** from their public presence, meetings (online/offline), reflections, and memos—stays **always on**, **evolves with the expert**, answers questions on the platform, and **facilitates** the human expert.

## Everyone is expert and learner

Members are **not** only “buyers” or “sellers.” Everyone has expertise worth exporting as a service **and** areas where they learn from others or purchase help. The product should reinforce **learning by doing** and **growing by helping**.

## User Personas

### Learner (also often an expert)

- Founder, operator, or investor who needs specific domain help (legal, fundraising, product, GTM, tech)
- Wants fast, trustworthy matches and low friction to book
- May also publish a profile and offer sessions in their own domains

### Expert (also always learning)

- Experienced professional monetizing knowledge through consulting
- Wants minimal friction to publish, get booked, and get paid reliably
- Benefits from AI-assisted discovery and, over time, agent-style support alongside live sessions

## Product Principles

1. **AI-native discovery**: Describe needs in natural language; AI matches to the right people
2. **Dual role by design**: UX and copy assume users can be **learner and expert** in different contexts
3. **Multi-platform parity**: Same core experience on web, Telegram, and WeChat
4. **Trust through structure**: Verified profiles, transparent pricing, two-way reviews
5. **Minimal friction**: Expert-style onboarding in minutes (AI-assisted profile from links)
6. **Fair marketplace**: Deposit model protects both sides; platform fee is transparent

## Key User Journeys

### Find help → Book → Review (learner mode)

1. Describe need via AI match or browse the network
2. View profile (bio, domains, pricing, reviews, availability)
3. Select slot(s) and pay deposit
4. Attend session (online link or offline)
5. Leave review after session

### Offer help → Get booked → Get paid (expert mode)

1. Sign up, connect social / context for the AI profile
2. AI-assisted bio and assets; set availability, pricing, session types
3. Stripe Connect for payouts
4. Notifications, conduct sessions; remainder charging per product rules
